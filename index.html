Kalasaradhi is a leading center of art studies in southern Kerala, India.It is an independent and private institution.Kalasaradhi Centre for Performing, Music, Cultural and Martial Arts is full name.It is an academy for training in various traditional art forms of India and Kerala.Kalasaradhi conducting different types traditional stage programs.It also conduct workshops for different subjects. The specialty of this institution is that various art forms can be learned at the lowest cost. Fee concessions and sponsored scholarships are provided to economically backward students.

In January 2022, Kalasaradhi was inaugurated by Honorable Mrs. Sunitha Vimal, Kerala's first classical dancer Judge and Industrial Tribunal Judge of Kollam District. The inauguration was held in the presence of Mr. Kovoor Kunjumon, a member of the Kerala State Legislative Assembly.The director is K Harikumar, a writer and theater artist. Class Coordinator Muthupilakad Rajendran (Retd Subedar Major in Indian Army).Muthupilakkadu (near Sasthamkotta, Kollam district,Kerala,India)is head of this institution.Kadampanad and Adoor are branches.

Kalaripayattu,Kathakali Bharatanatyam, Mohiniyattam, Kuchipudi, Kerala Nadanam, Koodiyattam,Ottanthullal,Chakyar kooth,Kadhak dance and Drama, Chenda instrument, Violin, Carnatic music, Sopana music and Music keyboard .Painting and pencil drawing are other important study for program.

Our Vision

Kalasaradhi is an independent institution.The basic objective of Kalasaradhi is to teach and propagate the various traditional art forms of Kerala and India.Orientation of traditional art forms to students of Kerala and India in modern times.

Another important objective is to teach art forms like Kathakali, traditional dance forms, ritual arts, music, musical instruments, etc. and the martial art of Kalaripayat at low cost.Kalasarathy strives to bring traditional art forms that are losing importance to the mainstream and find talented artists and make them prominent in their respective art forms.Aims at empowering women by learning art forms and performing them on various platforms. For that, various classes are provided for the convenience of housewives and working women in various fields.Kalasaradhi strives to fulfill the wish of those who were unable to learn art forms in their childhood due to various circumstances or who did not complete their studies.Kalasaradhi aims to preserve and teach traditional temple art forms. Kalasaradhi wants to create jobs in the arts sector and help underprivileged artists.
